Spastic Trees

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Notepad++

This is a Glitch art that was made using various portions of a single image of trees. The original image was altered many times by changing its code in Notepad. Once a good alteration was reached, I took portions of that and many other alterations of the image and compiled them all togther in photoshop.

The first main assignment of Art 74.
