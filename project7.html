Vibrant Sea

Tools: Adobe Animate, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator

Vibrant sea is an animated landscape that was keyframed in Animate with stock images altered in Illustrator, then exported in Premiere Pro. My first thought with this was to make a sea environment, as I feel like the way things move in the sea are so diverse that you could get creative with any effect in animate to make your characters move. I used the warp and distort transformations to tweak how the shapes moved, as well as a few other transformations.

The last project in Art 74, introducing Animation.
