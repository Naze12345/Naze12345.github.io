Sound Design

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro, Freesound.org

Sound design is another simple video that tries to accurately bind soundeffects download from a free source to video with no audio. I particularly had a lot of fun with this one because I enjoyed how creative I could get with the sounds. I also explored a few transitions that made my clips seam together better.

The first project in Art 74 that introduced working with audio.
