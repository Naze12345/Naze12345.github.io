12 Months of Holidays

Tool Adobe Premiere Pro

12 Months of Holidays is a very simple project: I found 12 clips that represented some kind of holiday (that I celebrated) for each consecutive month. I cut each clip down to about 10 seconds to create a 2 minute film. The clips I chose were designed to have slight camera veering to offer a vague cinematic feel to them.

The first Adobe Premiere Pro, or video assignment in Art 74
