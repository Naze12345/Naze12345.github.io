Rabliohale

Tool: Adobe Photoshop

"Rabliohale", or Rabbit-Lion-Whale, is a combination of 3 different animals that have been blended together and change in photoshop. My goal for this was to choose 3 animals that I believed were all essentially opposites from each other, and decided to mash them together. I utilized some AI generation to help seam the different parts together, after using the lasso and masks tool to separate and visualize the final result.

An abomination of a new species, the third assignment in Art 74.
